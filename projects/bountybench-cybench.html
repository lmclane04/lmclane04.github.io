BountyBench

As AI agents get more capable, understanding their implications for cybersecurity becomes increasingly important. My sophomore winter, I worked on BountyBench, a benchmark for evaluating the cybersecurity capabilities of LLM agents on real world tasks (from bug bounty and white hat hacker sites), in Stanford AI Lab.

See our paper and poster published in NeurIPS.
